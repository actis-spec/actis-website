# ACTIS Patent Non-Assert Commitment

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-08

---

## Licensing (repository and specification)

The ACTIS specification and documentation in this repository are licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0 (see the repository [LICENSE](../LICENSE)). You may use, copy, and distribute the specification under the terms of that license. The **patent non-assert commitment** below is separate from and in addition to the Apache-2.0 license; it addresses patent claims, not copyright.

---

## Non-Assert Covenant

The ACTIS Maintainers irrevocably covenant not to assert any patent claims necessary to implement the ACTIS v1.x specification against any party implementing the specification in good faith.

---

## Scope

This commitment applies to:

- The ACTIS v1.x specification (as published in the actis/ normative documents)
- Implementations that conform to the specification

---

## Termination

This non-assert commitment terminates only if a party asserts a patent claim against an ACTIS implementation specifically for implementing ACTIS.

---

## Implementation Rights

Implementers of the ACTIS specification have the right to:

- Implement the specification in software or hardware without seeking additional permission from the ACTIS Maintainers.
- Distribute, use, and sublicense their implementations under the implementer's chosen license, subject to that license's terms.
- Rely on this commitment for as long as the commitment is in effect and the implementer is not asserting patent claims against ACTIS implementations as described in Termination above.

This commitment does not grant any rights to trademarks or other non-patent intellectual property of the ACTIS Maintainers or their organizations.
